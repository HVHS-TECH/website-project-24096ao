Bee

Bee grew up in a small town in [redacted] with her mother and father. [redacted] was an anarchy and a military run country, this made it very dangerous for her and her family. When [redacted] started the war of units, her family couldn't get out of the country and Draxian troops were sent into her town and killed her family and she and 9 other people in her town survived. A charity organisation got her out of [redacted] and into Draxian where she was fostered, went to school and decided to become a private investigator. She moved to Brickport and joined the Keystone PI under Victor.

Victor

He grew up just outside the capital of Draxian with his 3 younger brothers and mother. He joined the military when he was 17 and met Will there. He was trained as an assaultman in the Rangers and is good at getting in and out of a bad situation quickly. He then moved on to Delta and was the commander of the eight other people in his unit. Some terrorism stuff happened, and his team got killed. He has survivor's guilt and doesn't trust the police and government because his team could have been alive, but the government didn't care, and they knew stuff, so they were sent there to be killed. He got kicked out of the military for not following orders, and because he didn't, he and I survived. He now owns a private investigator's business called Key Stone PI in Brickport because he doesn't believe the police care and do a good job.

Will

He grew up in a small poor town in Virjansk, with his parents. He moved to Draxian alone when he was 16 and lied about his age to join the military. He was a field engineer for the rangers and met Victor there. He moved around and was in the SOAR for a while before ending up in another team with victor in delta. He now owns a bar in The city, does crime scene clean up and occasionally helps Victor with cases.
