He/Him

Grew up in a smaller town, moved to the city for college, and in search of work. Finished college and started working full-time at Keystone PI. He would have joined the military instead of going to college, but got scared of the change in structure. He has done a couple of cases working in evidence inspection. On his last case, he was attacked, but was too shocked to react to the situation. He only survived the attack due to his friend Victor stepping in to help.

hobbies

Milsim LARPs, Airsoft. WW2 LARPs, WW3 LARPS


personality

He is a very structured person, relying on alarms and timetables to keep him moving. He isn’t good at processing changes to that routine. He was very shaken by his last case, as he thought his “Training” of milsim events would help him if he ever got attacked.
