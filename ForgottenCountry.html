The forgotten country

Bees county, which no longer is on any maps or history books, was a small struggling country, which was led by violent gangs and the military. The country was very poor and cut off from the rest of the world so they had to survive off oil, drug and weapon exports and imported pretty much everything else including food, the military and gangs hoarded all the nice stuff and weapons. Draxian sent troops over to help it because it was struggling but the military responded violently and the gangs also disliked them interfering with their empires they believed if they helped all their wealth and stability wouldn’t. They started targeting and bombing a few of the military camps and sent messages to the Draxian parliament that they should look after their own people. The military joined together with a couple of the biggest gangs and carried out quite a few terrorist attacks on Draxian government buildings that they had set up in that county which caused them to take their forces out of it but after quite a few more bigger attacks on Draxian soil they sent forces back to find the leaders of the gangs and military who had attacked them. They succeeded and ended up further destabilising the country. After the war bits and pieces of the land ended up falling and the surrounding countries but a lot of the land was declared no man's land because of the mines and nuclear fallout that made a lot of the land unusable. Millions of people were left homeless and in awful refugee camps where gangs used them as slaves the small military forces and police were violent to the civilians. Draxian had effectively bombed the entire country and killed thousands. The many gangs overpowered what was left of the military and started to turn on the government. The government, before it was overpowered tried to remove all mentions of it, the war and its defeat from history and draxian, in order to make alliances with other countries in the future helped to scrub all mentions of the war from history.
