Information

Nyetgeon, the world is very extreme and polluted, weather is constantly ranging from extreme to extreme, especially after WW4. Because of this most rich cities have installed a weather bubble, a swarm of drones that regulate the climate. The world has an extensive and grotesque history full of blood shed but also achievements, cures and people who are willing to sacrifice themselves to end wars. But war has slowly become less and less popular because the WPC has held a firm hand when negotiating land and products.
