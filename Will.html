Will

Will (William) 25

He/They

He grew up in a small poor town in Virjansk, with his parents who stugged with money. He moved to Draxian alone when he was 16 and lied about his age to join the military. He was a field engineer for the rangers and met Victor there. He moved around and was in the SOAR for a while before ending up in another team with victor in delta. He now owns a bar in The city, does crime scene clean up and occasionally helps Victor with cases.

hobbies

Will spends most their time working at the bar, helping with cases or doing crime scene clean ups. When he is not working he is usually reading, learning a new language or cleaning.


personality

He is really intelligent and quick-witted but quiet. He is not usually one to keep a conversation going and prefers to listen. He usually helps with some cases Victor and the others cannot figure out and can easily read people like a book.
