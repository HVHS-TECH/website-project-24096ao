William (Will)
He/They

He grew up in a small poor town in Raniton, with his parents who were abusive. He moved to Draxian alone when he was 16 and lied about his age to join the military. He was a field engineer for the rangers and met Victor there. He moved around and was in the soar for a while before ending up in another team with victor in delta. He now owns a bar in The city, does crime scene clean up and occasionally helps Victor with cases.
